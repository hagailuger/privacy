Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Classic Stopwatch app (the "App"), developed by LugerApps. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains what information is collected when you use our App and how it is used.

Information We Do Not Collect

Classic Stopwatch is a mechanical-style stopwatch timer. The App does not require account registration, login, or any personal information. We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Audio recordings or microphone data
  • Photos or files from your device

Local Storage

The App saves your preferences (sound, haptics, and tick-mode settings) locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • General location data (country/region level)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their policies. For more information, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by resetting your Advertising ID in your device settings.

Permissions

  • Internet: Required to display advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • Vibrate: Used to provide optional haptic feedback when buttons are pressed. Can be disabled in the App's settings.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob – for displaying advertisements. AdMob Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
