Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Soundboard app (the "App"), developed by LugerDev. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not require account registration, login, or any personal information. We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Audio recordings or microphone data
  • Photos or files from your device

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including device identifiers and usage data. This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their policies.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Notifications

The App may request permission to send notifications. This permission is optional — you can decline it without affecting the App's core functionality. No notification data is shared with third parties.

Local Storage

The App saves your favorite sounds and preferences locally on your device using standard Android storage (SharedPreferences). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
