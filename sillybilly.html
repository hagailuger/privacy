Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the SillyBilly app (the "App"), developed by Hagai Luger. SillyBilly lets you record your voice, apply fun sound effects, and share the result. We are committed to protecting your privacy and explain below what information the App uses and how.

Microphone & Voice Recordings

SillyBilly accesses your device microphone only while you are actively recording (after you tap the record button). Your recording is used solely to create the effected audio and video on your device.

  • All audio recording and effect processing happens entirely on your device.
  • Your voice recordings and the videos created from them are never uploaded to our servers — we do not have a server that receives them.
  • Recordings are kept in temporary storage on your device and are overwritten or removed as you create new ones.
  • A recording or video leaves your device only if you choose to share it using your device's share feature (for example, to a messaging or social app). At that point it is handled by the app you share to, under that app's privacy policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require an account, and we do not collect your name, email, contacts, or the content of your recordings. We cannot listen to, access, or store your recordings.

Analytics

The App uses Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how the App is used so we can improve it. This collects anonymous, aggregated usage events — for example that an effect was selected or that a share was started — along with general device and app information (such as device model, operating system version, and app version). It does not include your voice recordings.

Crash Reporting

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics to receive automatic diagnostic reports if the App crashes, so we can fix problems. These reports may include device state and technical information about the crash. They do not include your recordings.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements (which may include banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads). AdMob may collect and use information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, which may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • General location data (country/region level)

For more information on how Google uses this data, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Where required, we ask for your consent before showing personalized ads. You can also opt out of personalized advertising by resetting your Advertising ID in your device settings.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time purchase ("Pro") that removes ads and unlocks all effects. Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment details; payment is handled by Google under Google's terms and privacy policy.

Permissions

  • Microphone: Used only while you are recording, to capture the audio you choose to transform. Processing happens on your device.
  • Internet: Required to display advertisements, deliver analytics and crash reports, and process purchases.
  • Notifications (if enabled): Only used, with your opt-in, to let you know about new effects or features. No notifications are sent without your permission.

Data Security

Because your recordings are processed and stored locally on your device, the security of that content depends on your device's security. We recommend keeping your device updated and protected with a screen lock.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
